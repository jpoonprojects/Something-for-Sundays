Welcome to the club!

Your subscription is confirmed — a receipt is on its way to your inbox. Your first issue will be in the mail soon. Thank you for being here. ✨

If you've been reading this newsletter for a while, you know we spend a lot of time taking things apart. Critiquing systems. Asking hard questions. Learning to sit in the uncertainty. That work is real, and I'm not done with it. But this past year or so, I've been feeling something else — a quieter kind of curiosity. A pull toward practices and traditions and ways of reaching toward the sacred that I haven't explored yet. The rebuilding has started, slowly, without a blueprint.

And I wanted to do something with that feeling that feels more tangible and real.

So I'm launching the Something for Sundays Mail Club. A monthly zine — fully hand-illustrated by me — about wisdom traditions, religion, and spiritual practices from around the world. It's a small zine about big things, made for anyone: devoutly religious, fully atheist, or somewhere beautifully in between.

This zine will explore different faith traditions, practices, and ways humans have reached toward something bigger than themselves. Not to convert. Not to make any sort of conclusions. Just to wonder together.

Each issue is 8 pages of illustrations, insights, and anonymous reader questions (you can submit yours right here, by the way). Every single copy is created, bound, sealed, and sent by yours truly!
